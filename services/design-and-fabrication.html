What We Do

Taminje International Limited provides design and fabrication services built around practical solutions, quality workmanship, and dependable delivery. We support clients who need fit for purpose outputs—planned clearly, executed safely, and completed to specification.

Our design and fabrication services can support industrial and commercial requirements, including:

  • Design support and concept development (as applicable to project scope)
  • Fabrication planning and execution
  • Installation support (where required)
  • Repairs, modifications, and improvement works (project dependent)

How We Work

  1. Requirement review – goals, constraints, and specifications
  2. Scope definition – deliverables, materials, schedule, responsibilities
  3. Fabrication execution – controlled workmanship and quality checks
  4. Handover – completion confirmation and client sign off

Why Choose Taminje International Limited

  • Experienced leadership with disciplined delivery standards
  • Clear scope control and communication
  • Strong attention to quality and practical outcomes
