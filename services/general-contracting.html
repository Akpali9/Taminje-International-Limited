What We Do

Taminje International Limited provides general contracting services to clients who need reliable coordination and accountable execution. We focus on clear scope definition, efficient resource management, and disciplined project delivery from start to finish.

Depending on project requirements, our contracting support includes:

  • Project coordination and execution oversight
  • Resource planning and schedule coordination
  • Supplier and workforce coordination (as applicable)
  • Quality and progress monitoring aligned with scope

Our Delivery Method

  1. Project briefing – clarify needs, scope, and success criteria
  2. Planning – timelines, milestones, responsibilities, resources
  3. Execution – controlled coordination and progress monitoring
  4. Handover – completion checks and documentation close out

Why Choose Us

  • Clear communication and scope control
  • Professional coordination and accountable delivery
  • Experienced leadership supporting disciplined execution
