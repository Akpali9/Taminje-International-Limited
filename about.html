About Taminje International Limited

A multi-service design and fabrication company providing manufacturing and processing services, vegetable oil processing, consultancy, and general contracting for clients who value structured, professional delivery.

Who We Are

Taminje International Limited is a multi service company focused on design and fabrication, manufacturing, vegetable oil processing, consultancy services, and general contracting. Registered in February 2026, we were established to deliver practical solutions that meet real operational needs—combining technical capability, disciplined execution, and service excellence.

While the company is newly registered, our leadership is not new to the work. Our principal officers bring over 33 years and over 12 years of experience respectively, gained across public and private sector environments. This depth of exposure helps us deliver with professionalism, quality awareness, and a strong understanding of compliance, safety, timelines, and stakeholder expectations.

We are a solutions driven organisation built to support clients who need dependable project delivery, quality workmanship, efficient operations, and clear communication from planning through completion. Our approach is to understand the client’s goals, define the scope properly, manage resources responsibly, and execute with attention to detail—so projects and services are completed safely, correctly, and on time.
